# FAQs on Admissions
*Modified on: Fri, Nov 8, 2024 at 2:25 PM*



**I want to study at UPOU. Am I qualified to enroll?**
Only the following students are eligible to enroll at UP Open University:

Newly admitted students;
Continuing students;
Cross-Enrollee students; and
Non-Degree students.
For more information about enrollment at UPOU, go to the [**Enrollment**](https://registrar.upou.edu.ph/enrollment/) page.



**What are the courses/programs offered at UPOU?**

UPOU offers 7 undergraduate, 2 graduate certificates, 12 graduate diplomas, 13 master’s, and 3 doctorate programs. A Professional Teaching Certificate program, Continuing Education Programs, and Massive Open Online Courses are also offered.

Visit this link (https://www.upou.edu.ph/academics/) for more information about UPOU degree programs and UPOU non-formal courses.

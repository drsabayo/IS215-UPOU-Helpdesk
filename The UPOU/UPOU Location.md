# UPOU Location
*Modified on: Mon, Mar 8, 2021 at 2:56 PM*

The University of the Philippines Open University Headquarters (UPOU HQ) is located at National Highway, Brgy. Maahas, Los Baños, Laguna, Philippines.



To contact the UPOU HQ, you can call (+6349) 536-6001 to 06 local 710, or 536-5992. However, do note that until further notice, phone calls to UPOU may be unattended due to the reduced workforce.



For inquiries or concerns related to admission, registration, and other student concerns, file a support ticket in the UPOU Helpdesk (https://helpdesk.upou.edu.ph).

If your inquiry or concern is not related to admission, registration, or other student concerns, you can directly send an email to the UPOU Office of your concern. Check it here: UPOU Directory.

# UPOU Mega Learning Hubs
*Modified on: Mon, Mar 8, 2021 at 2:32 PM*

The University of the Philippines Open University (UPOU) has three (3) Mega Learning Hub offices. The locations of these offices are as follows:

Diliman - UPOU-DICT Building, C.P. Garcia Avenue, DIliman, Quezon City, Philippines
Cebu - Arts & Sciences Building, UPOU Cebu, UP Cebu Lahug, Cebu City, Philippines
Davao - Terraza Milesa Building, UPOU Davao, Andarizal Center, Cor Anda and Rizal St., Davao City, Philippines


The Mega Learning Hubs serve as learning space or venue in order to conduct activities both physically and virtually; and for students and faculty members to collaborate and become co-creators of knowledge. It also provides venue for student activities.

Our Vision for the University of the Philippines Open Univeristy: *Pamantasang bukas para sa magandang bukas*

UPOU is an institution of higher learning committed to open education as a means of enabling Filipinos everywhere to achieve a bright future for themselves, their communities, and the Filipino nation. This vision is best articulated in Filipino —pamantasang bukas para sa magandang bukas.



In line with this overall vision, UPOU faculty, administrators, and staff are committed to teaching and learning, research and innovation, public service, and governance and administration that are underpinned by the principles of scholarship and academic excellence, inclusivity and equity, responsiveness and social relevance, collaboration and community, and sustainable development for all.

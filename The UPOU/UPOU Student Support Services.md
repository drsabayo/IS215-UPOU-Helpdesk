# UPOU Student Support Services
*Modified on: Mon, Mar 8, 2021 at 2:35 PM*

Since our students our dispersed all over the country and abroad, the University of the Philippines Open University (UPOU) set up student support services to assist their concerns:

- Admission
- Registration/Enrollment
- Matriculation
- Financial assistance
- Delivery of course material
- Setting up of examination
- Use of computer
- Navigating the course site
- Other administrative matters
We also support our students through:

- Scholarships and Financial Services Program
- Learning and Testing Centers Development and Management Program
- Counselling and Guidance Program
- Student Activities and Organizations Program
- Student Accessibility Program
- MyPortal
- UPOU Networks
- Library Services
- Examination Services

# Sustainable Development Goals
*Modified on: Mon, Jul 3, 2023 at 1:29 PM*

As one of the constituent universities of the University of the Philippines, the UP Open University (UPOU) is fully committed to contributing to the achievement of a sustainable world in 2030 with the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) as its guide. As the country’s pioneer of online teaching and learning, UPOU’s primary goal as an institution is to be at the forefront of the Philippines’ open learning and distance education by providing wider access to quality higher education for anyone, regardless of their status. At present, UPOU constantly provides materials for open, distance and lifelong learning. It also continues to form and strengthen partnerships across the globe to develop and deliver more programs and initiatives.



For more information about this, visit sdg.upou.edu.ph
